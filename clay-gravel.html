Questions

A few common questions about clay gravel, driveway repair, material spreading, and road improvements.

Can you deliver and spread clay gravel?

Yes. Wolf Creek Farms can help with clay gravel delivery, spreading, grading, and access road improvement.

What material is best for my driveway?

It depends on your soil, slope, water flow, traffic, and current surface. Clay gravel, crush and run, and other stone materials may all fit different situations.

Can you fix washouts?

Yes. Washouts often need more than material. The area may need grading, drainage correction, rip rap, or better water flow.

Can you work around culverts?

Yes. Wolf Creek Farms can help stabilize culvert areas, improve entrances, and add material where water flow has damaged the road or driveway.

Do you only spread material?

No. Material work can include delivery, spreading, grading, shaping, repair, cleanup, and related site work.
