Helpful Details to Include

You do not need a perfect description. A few basic details can help Wolf Creek Farms understand the job faster.

✓ Property location
✓ Type of work needed
✓ Approximate size of the area
✓ Any access issues
✓ Drainage or water problems
✓ Photos, if available

Good Photos Help

If you have pictures, send them after submitting the form or text them to Russ. Photos can help show access, terrain, brush density, slope, culverts, water damage, structures, or material needs.

Even with photos, some jobs still need an in-person site visit before an accurate estimate.

Estimate Questions

A few common questions before requesting a free estimate.

Are estimates free?

Yes. You can submit the form or call 334-207-3331 to talk through the project.

How fast will someone respond?

Response time depends on the work schedule, but the goal is to follow up as soon as possible.

Do I need photos?

Photos are helpful but not required. You can submit the form first and send photos later by text or email.

Do you need to see the property?

Many jobs need a site visit before a reliable estimate can be given, especially clearing, drainage, roads, ponds, and demolition.

What areas do you serve?

Wolf Creek Farms serves East Central Alabama, including Notasulga, Tallassee, Auburn, Opelika, Tuskegee, Lake Martin, Montgomery, Wetumpka, Eclectic, Shorter, and Pike Road.

Can I call instead?

Yes. Call or text Russ at 334-207-3331.
