Questions

A few common questions customers ask before starting a land clearing or site development project.

How much does land clearing cost?

Cost depends on acreage, brush density, tree size, access, cleanup needs, and the final use of the property. The best next step is a free estimate.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. Use the estimate form or call 334-207-3331 to start the process.

Can you clear hunting property?

Yes. Wolf Creek Farms can help with access roads, food plots, shooting lanes, fire breaks, and general hunting land development.

Can you build driveways and access roads?

Yes. Wolf Creek Farms can build and repair driveways, hunting roads, farm roads, and access roads with grading and clay gravel work.

Do you work on drainage problems?

Yes. Drainage work can help reduce standing water, erosion, washouts, and long-term property damage.

How long does a typical project take?

Small projects may take a day or two. Larger clearing, road, pond, or site work jobs may take longer depending on scope and conditions.
