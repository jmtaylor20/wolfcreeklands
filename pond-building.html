How Pond Jobs Work

Pond projects need a site-specific plan. Soil, water, slope, and access all shape the job.

01

Review the Site

We look at water flow, terrain, soil, access, drainage, and the area around the pond site.

02

Talk Goals

03

Shape the Pond

The pond area is excavated, shaped, cleaned, expanded, repaired, or improved based on the plan.

04

The surrounding land is shaped to support drainage, overflow, access, and long-term use.

Questions

A few common questions about pond building, pond repair, pond expansion, and water projects.

Do you build new ponds?

Yes. Wolf Creek Farms can help with new pond construction depending on the property, access, soil, and water flow.

Can you expand an existing pond?

Yes. Pond expansion may include excavation, bank shaping, access improvement, and surrounding site work.

Can you fix pond banks?

Yes. Shoreline shaping, bank cleanup, erosion repair, and access improvement may be possible depending on the site.

Can you clear around a pond?

Yes. Brush, small trees, and overgrowth around pond areas can be cleared and cleaned up.

Does pond work include drainage?

It often should. Overflow and water movement matter because poor drainage can cause washouts or erosion.
