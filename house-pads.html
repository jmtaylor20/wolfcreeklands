How House Pad Jobs Work

A better pad starts with understanding the land, the future build, and how the site needs to function.

01

Review the Site

We look at access, slope, soil, clearing needs, drainage, and the intended structure.

02

Prepare the Area

The building area is cleared, opened up, cleaned, and shaped for equipment access.

03

Build & Grade

Material is moved, added, graded, and shaped to create a usable pad area.

04

Plan for Water

The site is shaped to help water move away from the pad and surrounding work area.

A Good Pad Should Work With the Land, Not Fight It.

The best pad is not only level. It also needs access, water control, enough working room, and a clear plan for how the property will be used after construction.
